VDJviz is a lightweight immune repertoire browser.

You can upload clonotype tables that were produced using MiTCR, MiGEC and IgBlastWrapper Rep-Seq processing software.

VDJviz server will compute a set of key repertoire statistics and display them as interactive graphics.

NOTE: Currently there is an upload limit of 25 files with at most 10,000 clonotypes, also your files will be deleted from the server after 24 hours

Click Your Files button to upload and manage uploading files, uploaded files will be shown at the sidebar
