The table below holds some basic statistics for all currently uploaded samples, including:

  • Number and frequency of non-coding (NC) clonotypes - clonotypes having a stop codon or a frameshift in their sequence.
  • Mean insert size - mean number of nucleotides inserted in V-D and D-J junctions for TRB, IGH, etc or V-J junction for TRA, IGL, etc.
  • Mean N(D)N size - mean number of nucleotides between V segment end and J segment start.
  • Convergence - mean number of CDR3 nucleotide sequences coding for the same CDR3 amino acid sequence.

Note that insert and N(D)N size statistics are only meaningful in case uploaded sample contains V-(D)-J markup information.
